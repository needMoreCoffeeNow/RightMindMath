Right Mind Math (RMM) is a free, open-source math app. It develops instant recall for simple arithmetic, and shows how easy math can be once simple arithmetic is mastered.

Here are some of the main reasons to use RMM:

  • Helps your engagement & interest
  • Free & Open Source — forever
  • Pretty darn well-designed app
  • 100% private

Item one (engagement & interest) is the most important factor at work here. Engaging with, and being interested in your child's learning process is much, much more important than the app you use. Fortunately RMM makes this easy & natural for you.

A series of Topics is below. Please take the time to read them to understand why RMM is such a great app for learning arithmetic.

RMM is a Progressive Web App (PWA) like Wordle. While RMM runs great in a web browser on every device, please be sure to install it as a PWA. (Unlike Wordle, RMM is a Free Open Source Software app.)
