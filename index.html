Right Mind Math (RMM) is a free, open-source math app. It develops instant recall for simple arithmetic, and shows how easy math can be once simple arithmetic is mastered.

Here are some of the main reasons to use RMM:

  • Helps express your interest
  • Free and will always remain so
  • Pretty darn well-designed app
  • 100% private

Item one (your interest) is the most important factor at work here. The simple truth is that your interest is much, much more important than the app you use. Fortunately RMM makes expressing your interest easy & natural.

An series of Topics is below. Please take the time to read them to understand why RMM is such a great app for learning arithmetic.

RMM is a Progressive Web App (PWA) like Wordle. It can be installed on your phones, pads, and computers. While RMM runs great in a web browser, please be sure to install it as a PWA.
